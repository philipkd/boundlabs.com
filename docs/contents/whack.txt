Between 2008 and 2012, Phil collaborated with creativity pioneer Roger von Oech to turn the best-selling Whack Pack deck of cards into chart-topping iPhone and iPad apps. The app was featured twice by Apple and is [available on the app store](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/creative-whack-pack/id307306326?mt=8&uo=4&at=10l74L)
